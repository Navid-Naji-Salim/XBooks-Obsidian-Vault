This is already **noticeably better**. The legend and the year labels fix two of the biggest issues. I also like the new subtitle much more.

The remaining problems I see are:

1. **The blue line should be a step line, not a diagonal.**

   * The balance changes *in* July, not gradually between June and July.

2. **The fake tiny green/red bars are still there.**

   * Zero activity should look like zero activity, not tiny transactions every month.

3. **The chart still has no value scale.**

   * I don't know what height corresponds to what dollar amount.

4. **The graph is oversized for the amount of data.**

   * One transaction occupies a huge chart. It feels sparse.

5. **The right-hand summary cards are disconnected from the graph.**

   * It isn't immediately obvious that the blue line ends at the **Ending Balance** card.

6. **The green status pill ("Cash position held steady in Dec.") feels unnecessary.**

   * It doesn't add much information and draws attention away from the actual data.

Overall, I'd say this version is a **clear improvement** over the previous one. The remaining issues are now mostly about refining the visualization rather than fixing fundamental design problems.
